Travelling

Exploring new places enriches my perspective and fuels my creativity. Whether it's the tranquility of the hills or the echoes of history in ancient cities, I find every journey inspiring. Some of my most memorable travels include:

  • Sajek Valley: Nestled in the hills of Rangamati, Sajek offers breathtaking views, serene cloudscapes, and a refreshing escape into nature.
  • Cox's Bazar: Home to the world’s longest natural sea beach, this destination is where the waves meet calm reflection and joy.
  • Rabindra Kuthibari: A historical site rich with the legacy of Rabindranath Tagore, where art and culture linger in every corner.
  • Panam City: A preserved city of colonial-era architecture, echoing the bygone days of grandeur and trade.
  • Mohera Zamindar Bari: A majestic estate with stunning architectural symmetry and a sense of aristocratic history.
  • Hajachora Waterfall: Tucked away in the hills of Khagrachari, Hajachora is a hidden gem where cascading water meets lush green trails — a perfect spot for adventure lovers and nature seekers.
